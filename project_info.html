General Aquarium Care

Proper setup and maintenance are key for both freshwater and saltwater setups.

Freshwater Tanks

  • Temperature: 72–78°F
  • Use dechlorinated water and live plants (Java Fern, Anubias).
  • Popular fish: Betta, Neon Tetra, Guppies.

Saltwater Tanks

  • Salinity: 1.020–1.025 SG; pH: 8.1–8.4.
  • Live rock & protein skimmer for filtration.
  • Popular species: Clownfish, Blue Tang, Mandarinfish.
