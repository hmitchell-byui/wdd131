Freshwater Tank Setup

Freshwater aquariums offer a peaceful and rewarding hobby for nature lovers and design enthusiasts alike. With the right balance of filtration, lighting, and plant care, you can cultivate a vibrant aquatic environment that supports healthy fish and lush greenery. Whether you're housing tetras, guppies, or shrimp, each species adds its own charm to the ecosystem. My setup focuses on low-maintenance plants, beginner-friendly fish, and a natural aquascape inspired by riverbeds and forest streams.
